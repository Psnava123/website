MAHINDRA: NOT WORRIED ABOUT TESLS INDIA ENTRY

The company has a great market share in the Indian market, and it has been generating strong profits in recent quarters.

Mahindras strong sales in July are a sign of the growing demand for SUVs in India. The company is well-positioned to capitalize on this demand, and it is likely to continue to grow its market share in the coming years.
